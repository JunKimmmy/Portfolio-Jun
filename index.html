    My name is Jun Kim.

    I am currently pursuing my Bachelor of Applied Science (BASc) in Electrical Engineering at the University of Waterloo.

    Driven by curiosity and a passion for innovation, I am dedicated to pushing the boundaries of technology and solving challenging problems. My primary interests lie in power electronics, PCB design, handheld electronics, and the rapidly evolving field of electric vehicles (EVs).
    I have a deep love for creating and experimenting, evident in my personal projects such as the Multimeter Business Card PCB, the BIONIC EVO: Prosthetic Arm, and an AC-DC Flyback Converter. Each project reflects my desire to bring concepts to life and contribute to practical, impactful solutions.
    I am well-versed in PCB design, power electronics, embedded systems, battery management design, and photovoltaic systems, and I consistently leverage my expertise in mathematics and physics to approach problems with a strong analytical foundation.

    Electrical engineering is more than just a field of study for me — it is a lifelong passion that fuels my creativity and drive to make a difference.
